About

I am an educational multimedia producer based out of Cambridge, MA. Currently, I'm a Lead Course Developer at HarvardX, Harvard's Online Learning Initiative. I am also pursuing an Ed.M in Technology, Innovation, and Education at the Harvard Graduate School of Education. I am part instructional designer, part video producer, and part science educator.

Previously, I worked with the Science Media Group at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. I produced online courses and educational videos about neuroscience, chemistry, physics, environmental science, and my favorite, astronomy.

Want to know more? Check out my portfolio and resume below.
